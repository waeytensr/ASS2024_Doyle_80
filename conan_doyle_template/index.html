Sir Arthur Conan Doyle - The Adventure of the Norwood Builder

Welcome to the digital edition (light) of Conan Doyle's The Adventure of the Norwood Builder, made as a project for the 2024 edition of the Antwerp Digital Humanities Summer School.

"The Adventure of the Norwood Builder", one of the 56 short Sherlock Holmes stories written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, is the second tale from The Return of Sherlock Holmes. The story was first published in Collier's (US) on 31 October 1903 and in The Strand Magazine (UK) in November 1903. (source: Wikipedia)
